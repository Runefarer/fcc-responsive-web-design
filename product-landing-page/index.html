Explore

Explore the Stolen Lands, a contested territory riddled with all sorts of dangers. Hundreds of kingdoms have risen and fallen in these lands, and now it's time for you to make your mark - by building your own kingdom.

Survive harsh wilderness, threats of rival nations... as well as threats within your own court!

Customize

Customize your character with a wide range of classes and powers, powerful arcane and divine spells, choosing from a multitude of class abilities, skills, and feats.

Create heroes (or villains) that for your playstyle and personality!

Characters

There are many companions and NPCs to meet in the game. Decide who to trust and who to watch carefully, as each companion has an agenda, alignment, and goals that may differ from yours.

Meet a diverse cast of iconic characters, including those from the Pathfinder setting itself!

Conquer

Carve your kingdom from the wilderness, with kingdom development a challenge alongside classic dungeon crawling and exploration. This is an adventure with diplomacy and court politics as well.

Conquer new regions and claim them as your own!

Rule

Your kingdom reflects your character and choices throughout the game; a living thing shaped by your alignment, allies, and ability to lead your people. As your kingdom frows, a number of factions and neighbours will come to you to seek favor - and to test your strength.

Grow your kingdom, as it reflects all your decisions and policies!
