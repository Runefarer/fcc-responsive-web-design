An amazing RPG experience

Pillars of Eternity is an extremely fun classic isometric RPG with a deep and well fleshed out world to immerse yourself in!

Pillars of Eternity offers an amazing story to progress through, in addition to a ton of more side-stories and quests that will keep you engaged for hours!

You start your adventure in the Dyrwood amidst a strange crisis affecting the people, having to navigate your way through different factions with different motives. Amidst the fantastic writing, art, and terrific music, you will get lost in the world!

Offers a lot of playstyles

You can choose your character from six different races, with different backgrounds, all of which affect the way your character will interact with the world of the game.

Pillars of Eternity offers conventional classes like Fighter, Wizard, and Priest, but also unique options like Cipher and Chanter! With a total of eleven character classes, you have a lot of different playthroughs awaiting you!

You can pick from the amazingly written companion characters, or hire and build mercenaries depending on how you want to play!

There are multiple difficulty settings, allowing you to configure exactly how you want to challenge yourself. And what a challenge it can be!

Hours and hours of content await

Pillars of Eternity is a deep RPG, with a long storyline and extensive side content that can be hours and hours of fun.

In addition, there are two amazing expansions, White March and White March 2, that offer their own amazing content, incredible story, and more fun companions.

At the end of Pillars of Eternity, an equally amazing sequel, Pillars of Eternity: Deadfire, awaits!
